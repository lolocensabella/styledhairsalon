Welcome to Girly Salon

About Me!

My name is Logan Censabella and I am the owner of Styled Hair Salon, I am 19 years old and I love the world of cosmetology. I am from NH and I am currently in school waiting to finish and brand my passion of owning my own salon. This is something I have been very interested in since I was a child. I am so excited to see where this journey takes me!

Since my earliest memories, the field of cosmetology has held a special place in my heart. From childhood, I was captivated by the artistry of makeup, hairstyles, and beauty. This fascination, nurtured by the vibrant world of colors and textures, has grown into a deep-seated passion that continues to shape my identity.

As I journeyed through adolescence, my bedroom transformed into a creative haven where I experimented with makeup techniques and hairstyles. The joy of seeing the transformative impact on a person's confidence fueled my desire to turn this passion into a lifelong pursuit.

Now on the verge of adulthood, my commitment to cosmetology has evolved beyond a mere interest. I envision enrolling in cosmetology school to formalize my education and refine my skills under the guidance of professionals. This is not just a career choice; it's a calling that propels me toward a future where I can share my love for beauty and self-expression with the world, helping others embrace their unique aesthetic.
